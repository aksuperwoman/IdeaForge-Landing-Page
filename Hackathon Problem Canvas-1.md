**Design Your Hackathon Solution**

Use this canvas to clearly define your idea before building.
The best projects start with **clear thinking, not code**.

**1. Problem & User**

**Who are you helping?**
(e.g., students, patients, commuters, small businesses)

→

**What problem are they facing?**
(Be specific. What is frustrating, inefficient, or unclear?)

→

**Why does this matter?**
(What happens if this problem is not solved?)

→

**2. Your Solution**

**What are you building?**
(Describe your idea in 1–2 sentences)

→

**How does your solution help the user?**
(What changes for them?)

→

**What makes your idea unique?**
(Why is it better than existing solutions?)

→

**What role does AI play?**

(Could this be solved without AI?)
